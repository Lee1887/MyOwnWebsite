About-Me

My name is Luca Lee Vincent Staudt and I live in Asperg, a small town near Stuttgart. I am 24 years old and currently enrolled in a web development bootcamp. I could never truly live and learn carefree because of some later diagnosed illnesses. I've been in treatment and receiving medication since last year in order to live a normal life. In the following texts, I will explain who I am, my interests, my goals (both personal and professional), and how you can contact me.

So be inquisitive!

MyLife

My life is somewhat chaotic. I was a very good and ambitious student until the eighth grade; I have an IQ of 126 and a "photographic memory." When I hit puberty, like everyone else, I became increasingly tired, limp, and unable to concentrate, and everyone blamed it on puberty. School became mediocre, and even my first degree did not go as planned. I was diagnosed with Klinefelter's syndrome in the spring of 2020, which means I don't produce testosterone, which manifests as fatigue. I was diagnosed with ADS in the summer of 2021; unfortunately, there is little help for adults, so you have to help yourself.My grades improved as a result of the medication, but I decided to discontinue my studies and enroll in a boot camp. I still have one more autism appointment scheduled. I'll keep you updated; my life is chaotic...but never boring.

Hobbys

My hobbies include open water swimming over longer distances ranging from 5 to 25 kilometers. A former classmate introduced me to ice swimming; unfortunately, Corona did not organize any German championships, but I did swim competitively a few times. Swimming in ice water is purely mental for me, as the water is 0-5 degrees and no wetsuits are permitted. Another hobby is private flying; I don't have a pilot's license yet, but I go flying once a month with friends and can already fly practically. History, old castles, and meeting friends from different countries are some of my other interests.
